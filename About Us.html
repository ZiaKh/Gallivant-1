Hi, I’m sushma mehta and welcome to my blog!

I’m a travel blogger based in North Wales where I live with my partner, Sam, and our three children. I blog about our life in the countryside and our mini adventures. I started blogging in 2021 as a travel blogger and I ran a blog called The Travel Hack. It all began while I was backpacking around Asia and I became hooked on travel and I barely stopped for the next 11 years! I travelled a lot….and then COVID-19 hit….and then I realised I didn’t want to be a travel blogger anymore.
So I switched things up, gave the blog a makeover and made it an adventurous lifestyle blog! I’m lucky enough to call blogging my full time career and I also write about creating lifestyle careers and working to live, not living to work. Sam and I have have 4-day weekends so we can travel as much as possible and enjoy time in Wales as a family. If you like the sound of the blog, please do follow along on Instagram for daily updates.
